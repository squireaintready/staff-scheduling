What it does

The product

  • Weekly scheduling grid — assign shifts per employee/day with reusable templates and one-click fill.
  • Payroll engine — daily and weekly overtime, lunch deductions, configurable thresholds.
  • Role-based — every employee has a role; schedule and payroll break down by role.
  • Reports & export — summary metrics, a pay-distribution chart, per-employee detail, CSV export.
